ABOUT US

HISTORY


ISCI Team is a community where the activists of Web Developers and Ethical Hacking are. ISCI Team was founded on the 12th of the 2nd month of 2021 and was initiated by 3 people. The initial goal in the form of ISCI Team only for Hacking community, then switch goals to become a cyber security and web developer community as well keep an eye on crime on the internet.

VISSION


Help and educate about the importance of cyber security and be careful when using internet and trying to introduce to the wider community about technological developments in the world Internet.

MISSION


ISCI Team aims to monitor websites, patrol social media, Supervise cybercrime and fraudulent activity on the internet.
